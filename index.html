VISION

To produce a Sri Lankan with a balanced personality full of knowledge and skills as well as good attitudes.

MISSION

To create a perfect citizen needed by the country in a planned and organized manner by making maximum use of physical and human resources so that the children who come to us to study will develop self-estem,self-respect,personality and creative thinking.

About Us

In 1940 Poojapitiya English School was started with 60 boys in a building roofed with plates collected from an auction held in polgolla army camp. It was conducted as a bilingual school. The first principal Mr. Kulathunga and Rev. Professor Hapugoda Nandarama worked immensely to uplift the school. In 1955 Poojapitiya Junior School's main building was opened by Minister Sir John Kothalawala. In 1980 this become a central college and in 1997 was turned into a national school.The classes are conducted from 6-13 Science, Mathematics, Arts,Commerce and Technology streams including 13 years vocational education newly started.
